Leagues - Tennis

Metro Tennis Group is New York's gay/lesbian tennis association. Through its annual tournaments, league play and competition, and themed social events, MTG offers the City's tennis enthusiasts a forum to play both competitively and recreationally, and an opportunity to make lasting friendships.

The group organizes doubles parties and other events including a singles ladder, annual club championship and annual national tournament the Liberty Open.
